Outinglicious

What is Outinglicious?

A simple app that helps you plan your next outing around Vancouver.

Inspired by the desire to learn more about Vancouver, and based on data on cultural spaces from OpenDataVancouver.

Functionality

  • Browse cultural spaces in Vancouver and save your favourites.
  • Create an itinerary and plan your trip with the help of Google Maps's beautiful UI. Explore the city in street view so you know exactly where to go.
  • Share locations with friends via Facebook or Twitter! Or both, let's share the love!
  • Create a profile page, because why not? No one else will see it, but you'll know it's there...
